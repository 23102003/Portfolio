Featured Case Study

Improving Inventory Liquidation Efficiency

Problem:
High inventory ageing was locking working capital and slowing down sales cycles due to lack of real-time visibility.

User Pain Points:

  • Sales teams lacked visibility into ageing stock
  • Manual tracking delayed decision-making
  • Slow liquidation cycles reduced revenue opportunities

Approach:

  • Analyzed stock ageing patterns across regions
  • Identified bottlenecks in inventory movement
  • Collaborated with operations and sales teams

Solution:

  • Built automated dashboards integrating stock ageing and NCO metrics
  • Enabled real-time tracking of slow-moving inventory
  • Designed targeted liquidation strategies

Impact:

  • Faster liquidation cycles
  • Improved decision-making speed
  • 4% incremental revenue unlocked

Key Learning:
Visibility and timely data can directly unlock revenue without additional resources.
